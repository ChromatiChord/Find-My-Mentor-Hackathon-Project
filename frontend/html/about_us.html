About Find My Mentor:


Strong mentoring is an essential tool in the development of a student, and the inability to acquire such mentorship can be lethal to a student's prospects. We here at Find My Mentor have the goal of delivering quality mentorship to underprivileged students of any age, whether they are of low socio-economic status, or are in a rural environment without proper access to quality education.

We also give this opportunity to more fortunate students, delivering it to them instead for a reasonable price instead of through charity. This funding from more privileged students allows us to deliver our quality mentorship.

We give those who wish to become a mentor the means of signing up, and after a strict vetting process (checking qualifications and/or industry experience), we give them the chance to deliver quality and personally tailored advice to a small number of the signed-on students.

Sign up today, and recieve exceptional mentorship from a vetted professional blindingly fast.
